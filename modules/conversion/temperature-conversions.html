About This Tool

This Temperature Converter is a versatile online tool designed for students, scientists, and professionals. It allows you to convert temperature values instantly between the most common scales used worldwide: Celsius (°C), Fahrenheit (°F), Kelvin (K), Rankine (°R), and Réaumur (°Re).

Key Features

  • Instant Conversion: Results update automatically as you type.
  • Bi-directional: Convert from Celsius to Fahrenheit or vice-versa with ease.
  • Scientific Precision: Supports Kelvin and Rankine for thermodynamics and engineering calculations.
  • Formula Display: Learn the math behind the conversion with real-time formula updates.

Common Formulas

Conversion Formula
Celsius to Fahrenheit°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
Fahrenheit to Celsius°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9
Celsius to KelvinK = °C + 273.15
Kelvin to Celsius°C = K - 273.15
