About Braille Encryption

Braille encryption converts regular text into braille characters, a tactile writing system used by people who are visually impaired. This tool uses standard braille patterns for letters, numbers, and common symbols, allowing you to hide information in a visually distinctive format while maintaining readability for those familiar with braille.
