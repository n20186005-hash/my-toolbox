What is the Random Letter Generator?

The Random Letter Generator is a free online tool designed to pick letters from the English alphabet (A through Z) without bias. It utilizes a cryptographically secure randomization algorithm to ensure every outcome is purely chance-based.

How to Use

  • Select Quantity: Choose to generate between 1 and 10 letters at once.
  • Choose Case: Switch between "Uppercase" (A, B, C) and "Lowercase" (a, b, c).
  • Click Generate: Instantly get your random characters.

Common Applications

  • Scattergories & Word Games: Need a letter to start a round? This tool is the perfect unbiased judge.
  • Teaching & Education: Teachers use it for phonics drills, spelling tests, and alphabet bingo.
  • Creative Writing: Use a random letter to start an alliteration challenge or name a character.

FAQ

Q: Is the selection truly random?
A: Yes, we use the browser's advanced random number generator to ensure unpredictability.
