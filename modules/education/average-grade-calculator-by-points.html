About the Point Grade Calculator

This tool is designed to help students calculate their current grade in a class based on a point system. By entering the points you've earned on assignments, quizzes, and tests versus the total possible points, you can determine your exact percentage and letter grade. It's updated for the academic year.

How to use?

  • Enter the name of the assignment (optional).
  • Input the points you received (Points Earned).
  • Input the maximum possible points (Max Points).
  • Click "Calculate" to see your total percentage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you calculate grade percentage?

To calculate your grade percentage, add up all the points you have earned and divide that number by the total number of points possible. Then multiply by 100.

Is this calculator free?

Yes, this online grade calculator is completely free to use for all students and teachers.
