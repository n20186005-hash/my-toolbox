About Random Card Generator

The Random Card Generator is a digital simulation of a standard 52-card French deck. It allows users to draw one or multiple cards (up to 10) instantly, ensuring a random and fair distribution every time.

Key Features

  • No Duplicates: When drawing multiple cards (e.g., 5 cards for a poker hand), the tool ensures no card is repeated in a single draw.
  • Standard Deck: Includes all 4 suits (Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, Spades) and 13 ranks (2-10, J, Q, K, A).

Common Uses

  • Magic Tricks: Practice mentalism or card counting without carrying a physical deck.
  • Probability Math: Excellent for teaching statistics and probability in classrooms.
  • Games: Forgot your deck? Use this tool for drinking games, role-playing games (RPGs), or board games.

FAQ

Q: Are Jokers included?
A: No, this generator uses the standard 52-card deck without Jokers.

Q: Is the shuffle fair?
A: Yes, we use a digital Fisher-Yates shuffle algorithm to ensure true randomness.
