How to Use Life Countdown

Click "Change Birth Date" to personalize the timeline and enable accurate calculations.

3

What is Life Countdown?

Life Countdown is a tool to visualize your time. It provides a grid-based interface to track your life progress.

How do I get started?

No sign up! Just enter your birthdate in settings to begin tracking immediately.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored locally in your browser (LocalStorage). We do not collect any personal data.

Can I use on mobile?

Absolutely! Life Countdown is fully responsive for phones and tablets.
