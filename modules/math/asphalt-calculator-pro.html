Calculation Formula

To determine asphalt tonnage: Volume (L x W x Depth) x Density. Standard Hot Mix density is approx 145 lbs/cu ft.

Types

  • Hot Mix: Durable for large areas like driveways and roads.
  • Cold Patch: Used for filling potholes and cracks, usually sold in bags.

Paving Tips

Always order 5-10% more material than calculated. Ensure the sub-base (gravel) is compacted and level.

FAQ

How much does 1 ton cover?

One ton of asphalt covers approx 80 sq ft at 2 inches thick. Coverage decreases as thickness increases.

How thick should a driveway be?

1.5-2 inches for resurfacing. 3-4 inches for new driveways (compacted).

Hot Mix vs Cold Patch?

Hot Mix is durable for large areas. Cold Patch is for fixing potholes.
