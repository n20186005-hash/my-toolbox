Notes:

  • Percentage change formula: ((New Value - Old Value) / Old Value) × 100
  • A positive percentage change indicates an increase, negative indicates a decrease
  • For "X is what percent of Y", the result is (X / Y) × 100
  • Percentage increase: X + (X × Y/100)
  • Percentage decrease: X - (X × Y/100)
