About the Easy Grader Calculator

The Easy Grader Calculator is an essential tool for teachers, professors, and teaching assistants. It simplifies the grading process by automatically generating a grade chart based on the total number of questions on a test or assignment.

How to Use This Tool?

Simply enter the total number of questions in the "Total Questions" field. The tool will instantly generate a table showing the score for every possible number of wrong answers. You can also enter a specific number of wrong (or correct) answers to highlight that row and see the exact grade immediately.

Standard Grading Scale

This calculator uses a standard grading scale, but remember that grading policies may vary by institution:

  • A: 90% - 100%
  • B: 80% - 89%
  • C: 70% - 79%
  • D: 60% - 69%
  • F: 0% - 59%

Why Use an Online Grade Calculator?

Back in the day, teachers used cardboard "slide graders" or printed charts. An online Easy Grader is faster, customizable to any number of questions (even odd numbers like 37 or 53), and accessible from your phone or laptop anywhere.

FAQ

Q: Is this tool free?
A: Yes, it is completely free to use for all educators.

Q: Can I calculate grades for 100+ questions?
A: Absolutely. Just type the number, and the table will adjust automatically.
