Technical Background Knowledge

1. Core Formula Explanation

The calculation logic of this calculator is fully based on the classic compound interest investment formula: FV = P*(1+r)^n + PMT*[((1+r)^n - 1)/r]. Each parameter has a clear financial meaning:

  • FV (Future Value): The total amount of principal + interest + principal and interest from periodic contributions upon maturity.
  • P (Principal Amount): The initial lump sum investment.
  • r (Interest Rate per period): The interest rate per period in decimal form (e.g., 5% becomes 0.05).
  • n (Number of Periods): The total investment cycle (years, months, etc.).
  • PMT (Periodic Contribution): The fixed additional investment amount at the end of each period.
