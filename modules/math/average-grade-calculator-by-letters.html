About the Average Grade Calculator

This tool allows you to easily calculate your Grade Point Average (GPA) based on letter grades. Simply enter the name of your courses, select the letter grade you received (e.g., A, B+, C), and input the number of credits or units for each course. The calculator will automatically determine your weighted average, which is essential for tracking academic progress in .

How to use?

  • Add your courses to the list.
  • Select the letter grade for each course.
  • Enter the credit weight (e.g., 3 or 4) for each course.
  • Click "Calculate" to see your GPA.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is GPA calculated?

GPA is calculated by converting your letter grades into points (A=4.0, B=3.0, etc.), multiplying them by the course credits, summing these products, and then dividing by the total number of credits.

Can I add more classes?

Yes, simply click the "Add Course" button to add as many rows as you need for your semester or year.
