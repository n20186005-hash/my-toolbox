Notes:

  • Square root of negative numbers is not a real number (use complex mode for imaginary roots)
  • Cube root of negative numbers is negative (∛(-8) = -2)
  • nth root formula: ∛x = x^(1/3), ∜x = x^(1/4)
  • For even roots (2,4,6...), input must be non-negative
