Q: Is it safe to lose weight during pregnancy?

A: Generally, no. Weight loss is not recommended unless specifically advised and monitored by your doctor. If you are losing weight due to morning sickness in the first trimester, consult your doctor.

Q: What if I gain more than recommended?

A: Don't stress! Focus on healthy eating moving forward. Sudden rapid weight gain can be a sign of pre-eclampsia (swelling), so always check with your provider.
